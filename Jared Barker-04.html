OpenTab

OpenTab is an application for you! The free app is designed to open you up to other users of OpenTab. This app creates randomly designated social sphere in the form of a bar enviroment. The app gives users a notification and all users have to do is show up. We understand that it may be hard to meet new people but when you can guide them with OpenTab, we eliminate the effort by bringing people together. We ask our users to do the talking.
